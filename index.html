A team of dedicated people

Auree gets down to business!

The team at Auree were once students not long ago, just like you. We understand how frustrating it can be for students or freshly graduated talents to get the job of their dreams, when the only reason why no employers would take you seriously is the notorious “lack of experience” excuse. Been there, done that!

Our internship placement program will get you to work as an intern for a company of your field of studies. Upon program completion, you will be exposed to real businesses and connected with other professionals in the industry, increasing your value in the labour market and bringing you more opportunities. Plus, all employers are happy to provide references for brilliant interns to use in their future job applications.

No lengthy online applications, no more weeks of classes and “bootcamps”, no more wasting time. Just you, us, and an interview with an employer who is sincerely keen on making you their intern.

The Auree's way

Result first, money later

We are not a non-profit organization, but we are not all about making profit either. We really want you to get results and be completely satisfied with the program first, so we have decided to make our program even easier to join:

  • Small deposit

    While the full service comes is worth $1000, upon joining, you only need to deposit 20% of the whole package, that is $200. This deposit alone guarantees you 3 interviews with 3 different host companies for internship placement. At this stage, we don't gain any profit yet.

  • Instant progress

    The first interview would be arranged within the first week. There are many occasions when our members get their internship in the same day of our initial contact. That's how fast our process is. Completely hassle-free!

  • Pay on commencement

    You are to pay the rest of the fee, which is $800 AUD only after you have successfully get an internship. If you are really unlucky, and doesn't get an internship after all 3 interviews, which rarely happens, we will do a full refund of the original deposit, no question asked. We will be sad though, as we were not able to get you the internship that you deserve, but we value your decision, and believe in total fairness.

  • Flexible payment

    We try our best to help as many people as we can, so for those who are really keen, dedicated and has potential, but with financial difficulty, we are willing to negotiate and come up with a reasonable payment schedule to assist you in joining us.
